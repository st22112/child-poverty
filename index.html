Child Poverty refers to a child who is under the age of 18 who lacks the basic needs of housing, food, shelter, water and money. It usually occuers when children are raised from poor families who are struggling to keep up with themselves. Children who brought up by poor parents are more likely to live in poverty as adults and the poorest children will highly die before the age of 5
